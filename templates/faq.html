Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and planning process.

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of event planning services including luxury weddings, corporate events, birthday celebrations, private dinner parties, and destination events. Each service is tailored to your specific needs.

How much does event planning cost?

Our pricing varies depending on the scope of your event, number of guests, and specific requirements. We offer flexible packages starting from KES 65,000. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events. For smaller gatherings, 1-2 months is usually sufficient. Contact us early to secure your preferred date.

Do you offer destination event planning?

Yes, we specialize in destination weddings and events. Our team handles all aspects including venue selection, travel coordination, and on-site management to ensure a seamless experience.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely. All our packages are fully customizable. You can mix and match services, adjust the duration, and tailor the package to fit your specific vision and budget.

What is your planning process?

Our process begins with a consultation to understand your vision. We then develop a detailed proposal, handle all vendor coordination, manage the event design and styling, and provide day-of coordination to ensure everything runs flawlessly.

Do you handle vendor management?

Yes, we have a network of trusted vendors including caterers, florists, photographers, entertainers, and venue partners. We manage all vendor communications and coordination on your behalf.

Do you provide day-of coordination?

Yes, day-of coordination is included in all our packages. Our team will be on-site to manage the event timeline, coordinate with vendors, and handle any issues that arise, so you can enjoy your special day stress-free.
